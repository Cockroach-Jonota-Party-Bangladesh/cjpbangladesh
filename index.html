Our Movement's Vision

Vision for a
Better Bangladesh

We are not here to repeat old politics or create new names for old corruption. We are here to ask simple questions — loudly and clearly.

Something is fundamentally broken. The system that was supposed to serve the people now protects those already in power. We see it every day — in the hospitals that turn away the poor, in the schools that teach nothing useful, in the jobs that go to connections not qualifications.

Our vision is simple: a Bangladesh where every human being is treated with dignity, protected by law, and given a fair chance to build a life.

  • 🛡️

    Safety for children, women & the vulnerable

    Equal protection and respect for every person, regardless of background.

  • ⚖️

    Proper healthcare in every area

    Close the gap between village and city. Medical care should not depend on wealth.

  • 📚

    Quality education for all

    Skills, digital learning, and real careers — not rote memorisation for certificates.

  • 🔍

    Transparent, corruption-free government

    Public money must go to the public. Full accountability at every level.

  • 🌐

    A modern, progressive nation

    Technology, innovation, and young ideas — Bangladesh is ready for the world.

Our Manifesto

The Eight
Demands.

No corporate sponsors. No political promises. Just eight things that should not need to be demanded — but here we are.

01

Safe & Human-Centered Society

Ensure safety for every person. Protect the vulnerable. Equal respect and dignity. Stop violence and discrimination in all forms.

02

Basic Human Rights for All

Freedom of speech, justice, religion, and movement — and full protection of every citizen's privacy and personal information.

03

Better Education System

Affordable, quality education everywhere. Focus on real skills, digital learning, AI, programming, and meaningful careers.

04

Healthcare for Everyone

Proper medical treatment for all citizens. Close the village-city gap. Affordable services, digital health, fast emergency care.

05

Jobs Based on Skill & Merit

Equal opportunity based on qualification — not connections. Fair recruitment. Support for youth, freelancers, and startups.

06

Technology-Based Government

Fully digital public services. Smart, transparent governance. Eliminate corruption through technology and accountability systems.

07

Corruption-Free Administration

Stop the misuse of power. Full transparency in government. Proper use of public taxes — at every single level, no exceptions.

08

Modern & Progressive Bangladesh

Innovation, research, and technology. Young ideas driving real change. A strong digital economy. A skilled, humane, forward-thinking nation.

Membership

Are You
Eligible?

We don't care about religion, caste, gender, or where you come from. We have only four requirements.

Req / 01

Unemployed

By force, by choice, or by principle. We don't ask questions and we don't judge.

Req / 02

Lazy (physically only)

The body rests. The mind overthinks everything at 3 AM. We understand completely.

Req / 03

Chronically Online

Minimum 11 hours daily. Yes, even during meals. Even in awkward silence.

Req / 04

Professional Ranter

Must complain with clarity, humour, and dangerously accurate observations about the system.

Membership is free, lifelong, and cancellable only by you.

No fees. No forms asking for your life history. No selfies with politicians. No "missed call to join" nonsense. Just show up.
